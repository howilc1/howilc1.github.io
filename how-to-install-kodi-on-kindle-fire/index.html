Next, head over to the Amazon App Store and search for “Downloader.” Install the app and launch it. In the app, go to the settings and enable JavaScript. Next, input the URL to download Kodi and hit enter. Wait for the download to complete.

Once the download is done, install the Kodi APK, and you’re set! Launch Kodi and enjoy a world of content at your fingertips.
