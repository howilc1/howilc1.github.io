How to Install Bosch Wiper Blades

The first step in installing Bosch wiper blades is to make sure you have the correct blades for your specific vehicle. You can check the owner’s manual or use an online resource to determine which type of blade you need.

Next, remove the old blades from your vehicle. Depending on the type of wiper arm you have, this can be done by lifting a tab or button on the wiper arm and sliding the blade off the arm. Alternatively, some wiper arms have a locking mechanism that requires you to press a tab or button while sliding the blade off.

Once you’ve removed the old blades, it’s time to install the new Bosch wiper blades. The process will vary depending on the type of Bosch wiper blade you have, so be sure to follow the specific instructions that came with your blades. Generally, though, you’ll need to insert the wiper arm through the hook or clip on the blade and then slide the blade along the arm until it clicks into place.
