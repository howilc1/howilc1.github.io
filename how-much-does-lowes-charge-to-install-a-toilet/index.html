Lowe’s Installation Charges

Now, let’s get to the main point. According to Lowe’s website, you can expect to pay around $90-$310 for toilet installation services, depending on your location and the type of toilet you choose. This price includes removing the old toilet, setting up and leveling the new one, and checking for proper functionality. Keep in mind that additional charges might apply if you need extra parts or if complications arise during installation.
