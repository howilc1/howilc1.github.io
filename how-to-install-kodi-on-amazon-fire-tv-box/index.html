How to Install Kodi on Amazon Fire TV Box

The process of installing Kodi on your Amazon Fire TV Box is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

STEP 1: Enable App Installation from Unknown Sources options on the Amazon Fire TV Box

First, you need to enable the installation of apps from Unknown Sources on your Amazon Fire TV Box. To do so, navigate to the Settings option in the main menu settings tab. From there, go to the My Fire TV option and select Developer options. You can then enable the installation of apps from Unknown Sources.

### STEP 2: Download Kodi on Amazon Fire TV Box

Go to the search bar on the home screen of your Amazon Fire TV Box and search for “Downloader.” Install and open the Downloader app. Once open, enter the official Kodi download URL and download Kodi for Amazon Fire TV Box.

### STEP 3: Install Kodi on Amazon Fire TV Box

Once the Kodi app download is complete, you can then install Kodi on Amazon Fire TV Box. Return to the Home menu of your Amazon Fire TV Box and select the Settings option. From there, go to Applications and then select Manage Installed Applications. Find Kodi in the list of installed applications and click to launch Kodi on Amazon Fire TV Box. Voila! You’ve successfully installed Kodi on your Amazon Fire TV Box.

To sum up, installing Kodi on Amazon Fire TV Box is a straightforward process that only requires following a few steps. Once installed, you have access to a world of unlimited media content that you can stream and enjoy from the comfort of your home.
