How to Install Addons for WoW

Before installing addons, it’s important to make sure that your game is closed. Then, download the addon from a reliable source, such as CurseForge or WoWInterface. Once the addon is downloaded, extract the files and place them in the Interface/Addons folder in your WoW installation directory. After launching the game, open the Addons menu and enable the newly installed addons.
