The Pain Points of Installing Cydia without Jailbreak

For years, jailbreaking has been the only option for those who wanted to install Cydia on their iOS devices. However, jailbreaking has many drawbacks, including the risk of permanently damaging your device and nullifying your warranty. Not to mention, many people simply don’t feel comfortable with the technical process of jailbreaking a device. This is where installing Cydia without jailbreak becomes a viable and attractive option.

How to Install Cydia Without Jailbreak

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it’s important to mention that there are many ways to install Cydia without jailbreaking your device. However, the most reliable way we’ve found is using a tool called Cydia Impactor. Here’s how:

Step 1: Download Cydia Impactor and Install it on Your Computer

You can download Cydia Impactor for free from its official website. Once the download is complete, install Cydia Impactor on your computer.

Step 2: Download the Cydia Installer .IPA File

You can download the Cydia Installer .IPA file from a variety of websites on the internet. We recommend downloading it from a reputable source to ensure the file is safe. Once the download is complete, save the file to your computer.

Step 3: Connect Your Device to Your Computer

Using a USB cable, connect your iOS device to your computer. Make sure the device is recognized and visible in Cydia Impactor.

Step 4: Drag the Cydia Installer .IPA File into Cydia Impactor

Drag the Cydia Installer .IPA file you downloaded earlier into the Cydia Impactor window.

Step 5: Enter Your Apple ID

Enter your Apple ID and password into Cydia Impactor when prompted. This is necessary to create a developer certificate that will allow the Cydia Installer to be installed on your device.

Step 6: Trust the Developer Certificate on Your Device

On your iOS device, go to Settings > General > Profiles & Device Management. Find the developer certificate that matches your Apple ID and tap “Trust”.If this option is not available then your iOS version is not signed anymore (check signing services).

Step 7: Enjoy Cydia Without Jailbreak!

Once the developer certificate is trusted, you can open the Cydia Installer on your iOS device and start customizing! And that’s it! Now you have got what you wanted!

The Benefits of Installing Cydia without Jailbreak
